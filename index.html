    UltraCortex

    A High Fidelity Dataset for Ultra-High Field 9.4T MR Images

    UltraCortex provides:

    • Large collection of images acquired with submilimeter resolutions of 0.6mm³, 0.7mm³ and 0.8mm³ using MPRAGE and MP2RAGE sequences
    • Lots of metadata and additional information such as brain masks, quality metrics, demographic information
    • Subset with manual segmentations of gray and white matter validated by neuroanatomy experts
    Still under development. Please check back soon for updates. Soon available on OSF.
